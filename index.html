    About me

    I am a second-year PhD student at Multimedia Laboratory, supervised by Prof. Hongsheng Li. I also worked closely with Shangzhe Wu. My current research focuses on facial analysis and generation, and 3D learning. I obtained my Master’s degree from Tsinghua University and Bachelor's degree in Tianjin University.
